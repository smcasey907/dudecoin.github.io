Dude Just Wants His Rug Back...

What's the Dude?

DUDE is a community-driven, decentralized token, created as an homage to the legendary Jeffrey Lebowski.

The Dude's laid-back, easy-going attitude and refusal to adhere to societal norms are what we were looking for in a crypto community, but that isn't always easy to find.

So, we made it...

Lebowski is the opposite of almost everything in the crypto space, and that is what draws us to the character. Where most tokens will try to convince you that they are the next sure thing, we are going to be honest and tell you we are not even sure what we are doing. How's that for transparency and honesty?

The $DUDE token is for people like The Dude.

DUDE is built on the Solana blockchain which allows for fast and secure transactions. The token was minted on Dexlab. The original liquidity was created as a USDC pair on Dexlab, then burned. Liquidity has since been expanded to a network of projects, most notably $FRN (Free Ross Now).

The token is available on JUP and Birdeye, and can be purchased with USDC or SOL.

How to Get Your Rug

Getting your rug is easy. Simply download a Solana wallet and purchase DUDE on one of the supported exchanges. Once you have DUDE in your wallet, you can participate in the community by voting on proposals and staking your DUDE to earn rewards.

Our community is open to everyone, regardless of their background or experience. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to participate in the financial markets, and we are committed to building a community that is inclusive and welcoming to all.
